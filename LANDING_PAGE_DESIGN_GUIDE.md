## üéØ Design Philosophy

### Core Principle: **"Show, Don't Tell"**

Instead of listing features, **demonstrate value** through:
1. **Interactive elements** - Let visitors experience the platform
2. **Real data visualization** - Show actual dashboards
3. **Before/After comparisons** - Highlight transformation
